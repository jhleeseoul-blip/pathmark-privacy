🗺️ Pathmark – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 30, 2026

Pathmark ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Pathmark collects the following data only on your device:

  • Step count data (via device pedometer)
  • Approximate location (only to suggest a starting city — never stored on servers)
  • User preferences (height, weight, daily step goal, language)
  • Journey progress (walked distance, completed cities)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To track your walking journey and calculate progress
  • To display personalized stats (steps, calories, distance)
  • All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third parties. All data remains on your device.

4. Permissions Used

  • Motion & Fitness (Pedometer): To count your steps
  • Location (optional): To detect your starting city. You may skip this at any time.

5. Children's Privacy

Pathmark is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
